FAQ
What's the difference between DeepSeek and Claude?
DeepSeek is the default model, paid for inside your subscription. It handles text, code, bash, MCP tool calls and multi-turn dialogues well. Claude is an optional upgrade: it adds vision (photos, screenshots), web search and long-PDF reading. Attach it with /claude inside your bot.
What does $10 include?
The subscription, the infrastructure, and a DeepSeek quota for everyday agent work. Nothing else to pay to get started. If you attach Claude, Anthropic bills tokens directly on your OAuth — we are not in that loop.
How do I connect Claude?
Send /claude in the chat with your agent. The bot replies with an Anthropic OAuth link — sign in, paste the code back into the chat, done. After that switch models via /opus, /sonnet, /haiku. Refresh the token later with /login.
What if my DeepSeek quota runs out?
The agent tells you and offers options: wait for the quota to refresh at the start of the next billing cycle, or attach Claude with /claude and continue on it. The DeepSeek quota refreshes every subscription period.
